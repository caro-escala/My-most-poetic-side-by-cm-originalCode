About Me

I hand over to you my voice and...

My most poetic side

Have you realized that humans are chasing the discovery of things that nature already treasures?

While our species is constantly improving in knowledge and cutting edge technology,

developing almost other-worldly stuffs,

Planet Earth shows us truly magic everyday.

I introduce you the series:

"Marvelous Dwellers"

This blog intends to make us closer to nature. That’s it.

I have this kind of romantic idea that if I do a good job sharing with you my insight about the world, maybe and hopefully, at least one of you could start to relish it in the same way as me. Perhaps after reading my inspirational tales, you will see the life around you with other eyes and you let yourself be amazed by it…

This is my way to invite you to be nicer with our marvelous Planet Earth.

So, without further to do, I hand over to you my voice and my most poetic side… I sincerely hope you enjoy it.

I will unfold this topic in a series of monthly deliveries.
